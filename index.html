I am an M.S. student at the School of Data Science and Engineering, East China Normal University, under the supervision of Associate Professor Xuesong Lu. I also work closely with Assistant Professor Chunyang Wang.

My research interests lie in recommender systems, with a particular focus on educational recommendation, sequential recommendation, graph-based recommendation, and multi-scenario ranking. I am interested in modeling user behavior, social influence, cognitive intent, and multi-modal information for effective and interpretable personalized recommendation.

  • LLM-enhanced Recommendation and Generative Recommendation
